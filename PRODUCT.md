# Runway

**What**: Obsidian plugin — task management over the checkbox tasks already in vault notes (Tasks-plugin emoji syntax). Two surfaces: a right-sidebar daily glance and a full-page filterable task list.

**Who**: Mario (PM / 0-to-1 builder) inside his marioverse.ai vault; potentially other Obsidian power users later.

**Register**: product — the design serves the tool. Density, scanability and zero-friction interactions win over decoration.

**Design mandate**: Runway is one of a nine-plugin suite (exo, masonry, horizon, superbasetags, notion-outline, node-zoom, …) that must share one visual DNA: built exclusively on Obsidian native CSS variables (theme-agnostic), compact UI-font surfaces, semantic state colors from the Obsidian palette (`--color-red/orange/cyan/green`), micro-transitions (80–140ms), tabular-nums counts, tracked-uppercase micro-labels for panel headers, accent focus rings. See DESIGN.md.
